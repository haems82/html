About Me

My name is Heather Yoo and a Web Designer.

I'm currently a full time student attending Art Institute in Santa Monica majoring in Web Design. Before becoming a full time student again, I graduated with Psychology major from University Washington and worked as a Qualtiy Assurance Specialist for few years. I have learned a lot but wanted more exciting and creative career so I moved from Seattle to California to attend school and is expected to graduate in March of 2016.

My goal is to build websites that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I want to add personal touch to your product and make sure that is eye-catching and easy to use.
